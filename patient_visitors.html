Patient & Visitors

Admission

We respect patients and their well-being as a utmost consideration. Our dedicated team of reception staff are available 24 X 7 to assist patient and attendant/relative with admission process. We always request patients to bring all necessary test reports, or any past medical history etc. based on the information provided, you will get personalised electronic record. CSH is500 bedded multispecialty hospital and we have various rooms like General A/C,Non-A/C, General Wards, Special, Delivery Suit, and Deluxe Rooms. Patient will be notified for the room rent and other care charges during the admission as per requirement. Further, you will be required to make an advance deposit payment. The Deposit can be adjusted against the final bill at the time of discharge. Deposit is also required to be paid on day to day basis (for cash patients). At CSH, you will find various cash TPAs/corporate credit facilities. Those looking for the cashless route would have to visit the Cashless / TPA desk for the hospitalisation of the patient. The staff will accompany the patient/relative to the agreed room/bed and make you feel comfortable. We do have terms and conditions related to cashless process. We do believe in fair practice of care.We accept all medico legal and Trauma cases.

Discharge

Our practice always remains straightforward during the time of discharge. Patient and Relatives need to consider few points like pharmacy clearance, TPA Approval, follow up consultation, pending bills etc. during discharge. Discharge process, which may take few hours to complete the process. Once your final bill is generated, you are expected to clear your dues by paying cash or by a credit/debit card payment, PAYTM, Google Pay etc. Patient/ Relatives should check all their belongings at the time of discharge. The nurse will hand over your discharge summary in your file. CSHdoes not take and responsibility of lost or stolen belongings. Discharge Nurse will also give patient/relative explanation of the medications that need to be continuing after discharge and follow-up instructions.TPA/Cashless patient’s final approval is required before discharge. Patient may have to wait until cashless/ TPA approval comes for the discharge. Approval can be delayed by TPA/Cashless company for indefinite time, patient may pay bill as per ‘cash patient ‘and get early discharge, CSH staff will assistyou for completion of documentation if needed in case of reimbursement. If patient requires any transfer facilities, communicate with CSH team during the admission. We do have a tie up with number of transport facilities, for local and inter-state transfer.
