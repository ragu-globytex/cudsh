Overview

Cuddalore Speciality Hospital (CUDSH) is one of Cuddalore District foremost multi-specialty healthcare providers catering healthcare services to patients in and around of province.With its state-of-the-art equipment and modern facilities, CUDSH is ensuring overall well-being of an individual is at the core of everything we do. Through CUDSH our experienced team of medical professionals, we provide quality and affordable healthcare to everyone.

Our Core Values

At CUDSH, we are devoted towards clinical excellenceand ethical practices. Our nursing staff and paramedical professionals are highly competent and provide great support to our clinical team Our healthcare services are aligned towards our patient's needs. Creating a compassionate and supportive environment for patients and their caregivers is utmost importance to us. Our patient-first approach has earned us the goodwill of patients through our ethical practices which is evident in our professional behaviour, morality, belief and confidentiality.

Our Expertise

We offer unified array of healthcare services from out-patient treatment and diagnostics to advanced clinical care which also extends to home-care services. We keep pace with the latest technological breakthroughs in medical science and equip our hospital with state-of-the-art facilities at par with universal standards.

CUDSH Ambulance Services:

Our Ambulance services provides 360-degree pre-hospital care during the golden hour in critical emergencies. Our Ambulance comes with critical life-saving equipments such as Ventilators, Defibrillators, Monitors, ECG & Other Emergency Equipments which aids to monitor critical patients. The earlier the patient reaches the hospital the better are the probabilities for any treatment to be effective. Beyond the first hour, the body’s compensatory response starts to fail and resuscitating the patient becomes more difficult. The patient is stabilized in the ambulance itself and gets the required care for he/she reaches the hospital.
